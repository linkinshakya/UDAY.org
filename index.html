What is UDAY?
United Democratic Awareness for Youth — dedicated to simplifying India’s Constitution and civic processes for young citizens.

About

Mission: Build a generation of informed, confident citizens by making the Constitution of India simple, practical, and relevant.

What we do: Bite-size explainers, case breakdowns, live Q&As, and campus workshops that decode rights, duties, and governance.

Why it matters: Constitutional awareness reduces misinformation, strengthens democratic values, and helps youth engage constructively—online and offline.

Programs

Constitution 101

Plain-language explainers of key Articles, Schedules, and amendments—no legalese, just clarity.

Civic Literacy

How elections, local bodies, and public institutions actually function—and how you can participate.

Case-in-Point

Real cases and current events made simple with practical takeaways and further reading.

Youth Dialogues

Live Q&As and campus circles that turn passive viewing into discussion and action.
